WaniKani - is a client app for WaniKani.com site.

Features:
 - Leaderboards and Achievements (compete with other users!)
 - Fast forward script.
 - Ignore button.
 - Smart resizing.
 - Notifications.

More features to come:
 - More user scripts
 - Kanji, Radicals and Vocab screens.
